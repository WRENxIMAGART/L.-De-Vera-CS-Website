Started in the year 2000 in the humble beginning of chairs, tables and tent rentals. Alfredo M. De Vera and Leonida F. De Vera both natives of Bulacan we’re equipped and blessed by the Lord with grace and ability to deal with business and celebratory functions…sooner at a fast phase their rental business expanded to establishing Food Catering business due to demand and constant encouragement from customers, friends, and family.


In 2002 rises the pursuit that led to on the way to a visionary achievement. L. De Vera Catering Services was established for the mission of Integrity and Excellence in Hospitality Service. The secret of their success in the catering business is that they have distinctive employees that are experienced with a track record of professionalism. Customers we’re truly satisfied and tantalized by the many delicious food options. Their price is the cheapest and affordable yet classy. Thereafter it enthusiastically drew people to spread and trust their service. Thus the ultimate reason it stands out with its name in the Catering Industry Business.


Today, L. De Vera Catering Services is one of the foremost names in the industry where they belong. It provides impeccable cuisine and exuberant service. Renowned for its attention to every single detail, promptness and excellent service armed with committed staffs that never cease to exclude warmth and grace. Regular training and workshops ensures their staff with service skills with emphasis on security, generosity, cleanliness and self motivated hospitality.


A professional event specialist and banquet staff ensures that every detail is met according to world-class standard. Clients had endless options, from flower arrangements to event hosting, packaged photography, planning and affluent functions and gatherings, birthdays, debuts, weddings, to name a few. They offer uniquely satisfying combination of Asian and Western Cuisines that will surely captivate even the most discriminating palate.


In the words of Leda De Vera… "I never dream of this but God gave me…a journey of determination and passion that leads to a successful business." That is the story behind the successful L. De Vera Catering Services soaring high for quality and excellence for God’s greater glory.
